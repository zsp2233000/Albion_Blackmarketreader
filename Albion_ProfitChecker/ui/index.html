FAQ

The most important questions, answered briefly.

How often is data updated?

Data is refreshed daily between 08:00-09:00 UTC.

Which regions are available?

America and Europe. You choose the region after login.

Is this official from Sandbox Interactive?

No. This is community-built and uses the Albion Online Data API.

Why do prices differ?

The API provides city prices and black market data that changes constantly.

Do I need an account?

Yes, login is required to keep region and account features saved.
