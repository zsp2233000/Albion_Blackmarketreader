# Retain all qualities, calculate the first three

**Status: accepted**

The local collector retains observed Black Market orders for Normal, Good, Outstanding, Excellent, and Masterpiece quality, including each order's quality. The first three qualities are included in the initial displayed-price and profit calculations; Excellent and Masterpiece remain available for inspection but are excluded by default so their weaker Black Market demand does not distort the item-level view while keeping future analysis possible.
